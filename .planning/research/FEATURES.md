# FEATURES.md — Product Features & User Experience

## Overview
The goal is to create a "Moneycontrol-like" experience: a dense, professional, and real-time dashboard that provides deep intelligence at a glance.

## Core Features (Table Stakes)

### 1. Real-Time Market Overview
- **Indices Ticker**: Live NIFTY 50 and SENSEX updates.
- **Top Gainers/Losers**: Automated list for both BSE and NSE.
- **Sectoral Heatmaps**: Visual representation of market strength.

### 2. Live Watchlist
- **Custom Tracking**: User can add/remove specific stocks.
- **Price Alerts**: Visual indicators for significant price swings.
- **Sparkline Charts**: Mini trend charts next to each stock name.

### 3. Multimodal Intelligence (The Differentiator)
- **AI Prediction Score**: A single 0-100 score indicating bullish/bearish bias.
- **Sentiment Gauge**: Visual representation of recent news (Positive/Neutral/Negative).
- **Technical Signal**: Summary of RSI, MACD, and EMA indicators.
- **Reasoning Box**: Short textual explanation of "Why" the AI predicts a movement.

### 4. Financial News Feed
- **Live Aggregator**: Aggregated news from Moneycontrol, ET, and Google News.
- **Sentiment Highlight**: Color-coded news headlines (Green for positive, Red for negative).

### 5. Historical Analysis
- **Interactive Charts**: Full-screen candlestick charts with technical overlays.
- **Backtest Results**: View how the AI model performed on this specific stock in the past.

## Premium Aesthetics (Moneycontrol Style)
- **Glassmorphism**: Sleek, semi-transparent panels.
- **Dark/Light Mode**: High-contrast professional themes.
- **Micro-animations**: Smooth transitions when price updates occur.

## Out of Scope (For Now)
- **Trading Bot**: Automated order placement.
- **Portfolio Tracking**: Linking to external brokerage accounts.
- **Social Features**: User comments or community feeds.
